## Pre-flight Checklist
1. Check if the drone's center of gravity is balanced.
2. Check if all payloads are securely fixed.
3. Check if all screws on the drone are tightened.
4. Check if the drone battery voltage is full (about 12.5 V).
5. Confirm that the USB drive capacity is sufficient to store this flight data.
    - The USB drive must be plugged into the flight computer.

:::warning Note
If a USB drive is not inserted, the drone will not operate.
:::

## 1. Connect Battery Power
After connecting the battery to the drone power, the motors will emit a short "beep" sound every second, which is the normal standby prompt tone.


## 2. Unlock Drone
Switch the emergency power-off switch to the **ON** position to power on the flight controller.


:::caution
If the emergency power-off switch is not switched to ON, the drone will not start.
:::

## 3. Wait for Flight Controller to Start
The flight controller takes about 2 minutes to start. When startup is complete, the motors will emit a long "beep" sound, indicating that the flight controller is ready.
