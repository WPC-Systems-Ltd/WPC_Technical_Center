# cPCI 32ch-2W MUX

## 1. Introduction

---

The cPCI 32ch-2W MUX is a 1-to-32 channel 2-wire multiplexer.

## 2. Features

---

*   Channels: 32 Channels (2-wire)
*   Current Load: Each PIN can withstand up to 1A current.
*   Application: Low contact resistance characteristics make it very suitable for automated measurement applications.
*   Expandability: Two cPCI 32ch-2W MUX cards can be combined to form a 1-to-32ch 4-wire measurement system.

### Instructions

---

When using, simply connect the +/- terminals of the instrument to COM +/-, and connect Ch_X +/- to the DUT pins respectively, enabling a set of instruments to measure up to 32 DUTs.
