# 16ch Voltage Attenuator

The WPC 16ch Voltage Attenuator is a high-density signal processing front-end module featuring 16 independent voltage attenuation channels. This product is specifically designed to step down high-voltage signals proportionally to low-voltage signals, facilitating safe and accurate acquisition by back-end measurement systems (such as DAQ).

### Key Features
*   **Precise Attenuation Ratio**: Fixed gain (Gain) of 1/3, enabling precise downscaling of input signals.
*   **High-Bandwidth Measurement**: Bandwidth up to 1MHz, suitable for high-speed switching signals and transient response observations.
*   **High Channel Density**: A single module provides 16 channels, effectively reducing system volume and wiring complexity.
*   **Wide Range Power Supply**: Supports a wide power supply range from 5V to 30V, with a typical value of 24VDC.
*   **Industrial Grade Environmental Adaptation**: Operating temperature range from -40 to 85 °C, suitable for harsh industrial environments.

## Product Specifications and Features

The WPC 16ch Voltage Attenuator provides stable and high-speed voltage attenuation. Its main specifications are as follows:

*   **Power Input**: 5 to 30 VDC (Typical value 24 VDC)
*   **Attenuation Ratio (Gain)**: 1/3
*   **Channel Count**: 16 channels
*   **Bandwidth**: 1 MHz
*   **Input Voltage Range**: Vcc - 0.2 V
*   **Output Voltage Range**: Vcc - 0.2 V
*   **Insulation Type**: Non-isolated
*   **Operating Temperature**: -40 to 85 °C
