### Product Features
---

1. USB 2.0 Full-Speed Bus Powered
2. 20-channel 3.3V Digital I/O (5V Tolerant)
3. Supports I2C/SPI/UART/PWM/Counter
4. Analog Input (AI): 8-channel Simultaneous Sampling & 16-bit Single-ended Resolution
5. Max Sampling Rate: 16kHz (Single Channel)
6. Input Range: +/-10V
7. Supports [Python](https://wpc-systems-ltd.github.io/WPC_Python_driver_release/), [C#](https://wpc-systems-ltd.github.io/WPC_CSharp_driver_release/), and [LabVIEW](https://wpc-systems-ltd.github.io/WPC_LabVIEW_driver_release/)
