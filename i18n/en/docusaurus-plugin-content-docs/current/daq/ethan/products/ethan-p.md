Ethan-P provides **4-channel 10-bit digital potentiometers** with a resistance range of **300Ω ~ 50kΩ** and an update rate of **500Hz**. It allows for programmable control of resistance values, suitable for analog circuit tuning and calibration applications.

### Key Features

---

1. Power input: 24VDC
2. 10/100 Base-T Ethernet interface
3. 4-channel variable digital potentiometers & 10-bit resolution
4. Output range: 300Ω to 50kΩ
5. Maximum update rate: 500Hz
6. Supports [Python](https://wpc-systems-ltd.github.io/WPC_Python_driver_release/), [C#](https://wpc-systems-ltd.github.io/WPC_CSharp_driver_release/), and [LabVIEW](https://www.wpc.com.tw)
